6.7/10

Should you watch it?

If you are obsessed with Jude Law as much as we are, this film is totally for you! Just imagine 107 minutes of heaven watching how Jude Law walking in his perfect siut, drinking champangne and being absolutely charming.

Did you know something about the director of The nest? Sean Durkin, who you are?
First of all The nest is his first movie where he is a director. He was a part of such famous projects as Martha Marcy May Marlene, Mary last seen and new The Rental.
The nest is a beautiful drama with a hint of a horror, that makes it even tastier. So we think that The nest is a perfect debut for our Sean and we are definitely looking foward to see new his projects! Congrats!
On review aggregator Rotten Tomatoes, the film holds an approval rating of 88% based on 92 reviews, with an average rating of 7.45/10. The website's critics consensus reads: "An effective pairing of period setting and timeless themes, The Nest wrings additional tension out of its unsettling story with an outstanding pair of lead performances." On Metacritic, the film holds a weighted average score of 79 out of 100, based on 25 critics, indicating "generally favorable reviews". The Nest was screened at the 2020 Deauville American Film Festival where it won the Grand Special Prize, the International Critics' prize and the Revelation Prize.
